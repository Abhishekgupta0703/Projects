ABOUT US

How familiar are you with your coworkers, their skill sets, passions, and career ambitions..?

The Leading Navigators will get your answers by building strong positive relations among your employees in hopes of boosting their collaborative tendencies by creating customized hours out of your daily office routine.These hours include Team Building Activities, Employee Engagement Programs, Soft Skill & Behavioral Trainings, Leadership Programs, Motivational Sessions, Off-site Activities and many more to present.

Our Creators have done programs with Powergrid, CBI Academy, PAC Task Force, ABP News, Mahindra & Mahindra, Amway, Spicejet, Indigo, Hitachi, HP, UP Police, Delhi Police, Vestige, BMW, Apollo Hospitals, MAX Healthcare, Fortis Hospitals, SIS Securities.

Our Story

One Day Miss & Mr. Founder were hosting a corporate award event as anchors, while doing this they noticed that employees of the same organization don't even know each other by name because of different departments.
Then they thought that most of the time once they get to be with working professionals, who should become family but everybody knows the name of only their laptops, not the people working with. That's a lack of Engagement, Team bond , Leadership , Mentorship, Motivation and then finally they decided to deliver the connection between employees by offering Employee Engagement, Team Building, Trainings & Wellness Programs.
